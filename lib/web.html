how to play the delotto

welcome to delotto, where you can bet on actors' punctuality to rehearsals.

1. account creation: quickly sign up in the top-right with a username/password to start betting.

2. points system: easily view your points balance and track your betting history in the top-right.

3. viewing past rounds: conveniently check the history on the main page and see ongoing rounds.

4. wagering: click the 'wager' button on an open round and place bets on whether an actor arrives over/under the 'line time', which is automatically generated based on past data.

5. monitoring bets: observe what others are betting on to inform your decisions by looking at the 'calls' column.

6. outcome: win points if your bet is correct, lose them if not.
